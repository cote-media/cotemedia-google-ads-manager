# Government / Public-Authority Data Request Policy

**Adopted:** 2026-06-11
**Owner:** Cote Media LLC (operator of LoraMer)
**Scope:** All requests from any government body, law-enforcement agency, court,
regulator, or other public authority seeking user personal data held by LoraMer.

Cote Media treats user data as a trust. We respond to public-authority requests
for that data only as legally required, and we resist requests that are invalid,
improper, or overbroad. This policy governs how every such request is handled.

## 1. Legality review
Every request is reviewed for legal validity — proper legal process, jurisdiction,
and scope — before any response is given. No user personal data is disclosed on
the basis of an informal, voluntary, or unverified request. Requests that are
invalid or improper are refused.

## 2. Challenge
Requests we believe to be unlawful, overbroad, or otherwise improper will be
challenged. This includes seeking to narrow or quash the request through the
appropriate legal channels. Legal counsel is engaged as needed.

## 3. Data minimization
Where disclosure is legally compelled, we disclose only the minimum data
necessary to comply with the valid request — never more than the legal process
requires.

## 4. Documentation
Every request is documented and retained, including: the request itself, the
legal reasoning applied, the parties involved, and the response given.

## 5. User notification
Where lawful, affected users will be notified of requests concerning their data.

---
*This is an internal operating policy of Cote Media LLC. It governs our conduct;
it does not create third-party rights or legal obligations beyond those imposed
by applicable law.*
